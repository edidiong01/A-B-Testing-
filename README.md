# A/B Testing - Marketing Campaign Analysis 
 To maximize return on investment (ROI) for our clientsâ€™ advertising spend, we conducted an A/B test comparing the performance of two major digital advertising platforms: Facebook Ads and Google AdWords. The objective of this experiment was to evaluate which platform delivers superior results across key performance indicators, including click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, cost per click (CPC), cost per acquisition (CPA), and overall cost-effectiveness.

The findings from this analysis will inform budget allocation decisions, campaign optimization strategies, and future media planning, enabling us to drive better outcomes and higher ROI for our clients.
